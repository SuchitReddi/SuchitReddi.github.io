KWoC'23

This is an unique experience where I got to mentor other students contributing to their first open source projects.
Two of my projects: Memory Wipe and Cyber Sentinel were selected for KWoC'23.
I mentored students interested in contributing to these projects by instructing them the basic structure of each project.
I also helped them in understanding the codebase and how to contribute to the projects.
One of the students I mentored was recognized as a top contributor in the KWoC'23 event.
